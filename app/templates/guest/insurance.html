Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How old do I have to be to purchase insurance?

You must be at least 18 years old to purchase most insurance policies. However, some plans, like life or health insurance, can be acquired by a parent or guardian for minors.

2. What is ChildCare Insurance?

ChildCare Insurance provides financial protection for expenses related to raising a child, including medical care and education, ensuring your child's future is secured.

3. Do you accept credit card payments for insurance premiums?

Yes, we accept all major credit cards for insurance premium payments. We aim to make your payment experience seamless and convenient.

4. How do insurance plans work?

Insurance plans provide financial protection by covering specific risks. You pay regular premiums, and in return, the insurance company compensates you in the event of covered losses.

5. Who is eligible for the Future Insurance Plan?

The Future Insurance Plan is available to individuals aged 18-65, and it offers flexible terms to suit various life stages and financial goals.

6. Why is it important to have insurance?

Insurance protects you from unexpected financial burdens. It offers peace of mind by covering risks related to health, life, property, and more, ensuring you and your loved ones are financially secure.
